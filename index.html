Frequently Asked Questions

What is RateMe.com?

RateMe.com is a platform that uses machine learning to analyze emotional responses from viewers while they watch movies, helping to predict movie ratings based on emotion.

How does the movie rating prediction work?

Our system analyzes your emotional reactions to movies through a camera, providing an accurate rating prediction based on your feelings throughout the film.

How accurate is the system?

While the system provides a prediction based on emotional responses, the accuracy depends on various factors, such as lighting, camera quality, and how well emotions are detected.
